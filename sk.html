My work for a Fortune 100 company includes the Knowledge Integration system, which is a centralized tracking application for customer work orders, invoices, inventory tracking, and more. KI gives both customers and employees direct visibility into their business operations, and allows those on the ground to quickly track and monitor orders in real time. The application handles various complex combination of data states, and allows for maximum flexibility in how orders are tracked. My work on the application includes co-leading a team of four developing a new feature to track a new type of equipment, migrating the system from local login to Okta identity management, and updating the Dispatcher subcomponent to work with the latest features within KI.
